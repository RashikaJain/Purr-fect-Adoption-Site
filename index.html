Tips for bringing home a newly adopted pet

⇒ When you adopt, you are making a commitment with the dog for an entire lifetime. You will be the primary caregiver for your pet. Talk to your family.

⇒ Choose a pet appropriate to your living situation. Consider the size, age and energy level of the pet so that both of you are a good match.

⇒ Regular exercise, stimulation for the pet and quality time together are a must!

⇒ The well being of your pet has to be considered when making important life decisions like relocating to another city, having children, travelling etc.
